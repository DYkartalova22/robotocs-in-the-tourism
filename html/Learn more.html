How technology will affect the tourism industry?

New technologies will affect every aspect of travel companies- from internal organization and management to how consumers will perceive the company, the quality of the services it provides and how it cooperates or competes with other companies in the market. In a new reality, where services are provide from machines, the roles of the participants in the whole process will have to be significant change. Greater automation means a greater role for consumers in the process of service because now they will have to perform some of the actions themselves. The use of self-service technologies also transfers responsibility for the start and the service from the provider to client- so users are transformed into Prosmers ( = producers + consumers) or co-producers and consumers. The introduction of new technologies will also require redrawing the service process itself- new processes, activities, procedures, control, create new instructions to reflect the new way of providing the service, as well as so new way of planning the operational activity. And last but not least, her robots automated technologies will improve the sustainable side of the operational activity on environmental protection and nature. The use of new technologies will reduce the amount of resources used, will there is less waste, it will be eliminated unnecessary actions will be consumed less energy and labor.
